SalmoNet

An integrated multi-layered network of the pathogen Salmonella

Browse Download

Features:

  • Manually curated dataset of regulatory connections in Salmonella
  • Interactions for 11 different Salmonella strains classified as being gastro-intestinal or extra-intestinal
  • Integrated resource with both high-throughput and low-throughput transcriptional regulatory interactions
  • Contains possible post-translational connections
  • Predicted genome-wide transcriptional regulatory interactions point to novel functions for transcription factors
  • Strain specific and consensus networks
  • Can be downloaded in a user-specified content and format
